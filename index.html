Micah Berman

Building technology and teams to help humans create a better world.

I'm a product leader who turns frontier technology into products that make our world better for real people — at Google Search, Google.org, Android, and now Ezra, where I'm building modern benefits for frontline workers.

I care as much about how we get somewhere as where we end up. I've taught graduate engineering students at Columbia, took a gap year to run a Habitat for Humanity affiliate in rural Mississippi before college, and built my first webpage at age 7 during a visit to my grandparents.

Outside of work I'm usually planning a wilderness trip, training for something endurance-related, or flying a small plane. I haven't yet figured out how to do all three at once.
